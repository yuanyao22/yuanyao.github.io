I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Environmental Health Sciences at the UCLA Fielding School of Public Health. I received my doctoral degree in environment and health from Peking University in 2022.

My research focuses on the intersection of air pollution, climate change, and human health. I use exposure assessment, molecular epidemiology, multi-omics approaches, and community-engaged research to study how environmental exposures affect human health. My work aims to translate scientific evidence into practical solutions that reduce health disparities and support equitable energy transitions.
